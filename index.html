1. Research Showcase

Welcome to our Poetry Competition on the theme of "Vanished Muse." We invite you to explore the depths of inspiration, mystery, and imagination as you craft a poem that embodies the essence of a muse that has disappeared into the shadows.

Rules

  1. Theme: Submissions must revolve around the theme of a "vanished muse." Interpret the concept in your unique way, exploring the emotions, thoughts, and experiences associated with the disappearance of inspiration.

  2. Length: Poems must be between 8 to 20 lines. Conciseness is key, so make every word count in conveying the essence of your vanished muse.

  3. Originality: Submissions must be original and not previously published. Plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.

  4. Submission Format: Submit your poem in a PDF document and strictly handwritten. Ensure your entry is formatted clearly and is easy to read.

  5. Deadline: The deadline for submissions is 3rd February. Late entries will not be considered.

  6. Judging: Entries will be judged based on creativity, adherence to the theme, deep emotions and overall poetic merit.

  7. Prizes: The winner will receive exciting prizes. Additionally, the winning poem will be featured on our website and social media platforms.

  8. Submission: Please send your entries to the below with your registration . Include your name, college , contact information, and the title of your poem as the document name.

  9. By participating, you agree to abide by these rules
    any decisions made by the judging panel. We look forward to reading your captivating poems on the theme of the "Vanished Muse." Good luck
